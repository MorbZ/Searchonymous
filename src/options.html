You may allow Searchonymous to send specific cookies to Google searches in order to improve your user experience. Cookies that carry your account information will always be blocked by Searchonymous.

Search Preferences Cookie

The "NID"-cookie is according to Google's privacy policy only used to store your search preferences. However as it carries a unique ID it is technically possible for Google to track your searches. You may want to allow this cookie if you change your search settings.

Consent Cookie

The consent cookie stores information of whether you accepted Google's (privacy) policies. In some regions a large message box will be shown on the Google search page if this cookie is not set. This cookie doesn't carry a unique ID and is therefore harmless in regards of tracking.
